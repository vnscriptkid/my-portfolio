What are my values?

These are what I am striving toward everyday as well as foundation for all of my decisions

Beggar for knowledge

I consider myself as life long learner and believe learning is the key to wisdom. If you have wisdom, you will be more happy, suffer less and live a fearless life. I read books, travel and learn from others.

Reach potential

We all have potential to do great thing, I believe there's no limit of human's potential and everyone is unique. If you really want to do something, the whole universe will conspire for you to get it

Help Others

I've always consider a meaningful life as a life of giving. I want when I'm 70, looking back I can realize that I've spend my whole life to make other's life better. My goal is to share my knowledge with others and inspire them to get out of their comfort zone.

Make an impact

I've decided to live a life in which I make an impact, not a superficial life. I want to create value out of what I do and don't strive for appearance. For me, money is just a tool and never be a purpose
